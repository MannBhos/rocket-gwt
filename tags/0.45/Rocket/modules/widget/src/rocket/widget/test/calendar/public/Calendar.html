Calendar

The Calendar is a simple widget that shows a month worth of days in a grid with each week allocated a row. Developers may then decorate it with buttons which allow the user to navigate forward or backward one month at a time etc. For more details refer to the javadoc.

Things to test

  • Each day has a click listener which when clicked shows an alert that gives the full date of that day.
  • Previous month / Next month may be used to move backwards or forwards a month
  • CalendarTest adds two styles that will make a weekend day yellow.
  • Days belonging to the previous month should have a pinkish background.
  • Days belonging to the current month will have their day of the month in bold
  • Days belonging to the next month should have a cyan background.
  • Today will have a green background
  • Click on the calendar icon to pop up a mini calendar (DatePicker). Click on any day to close it.
