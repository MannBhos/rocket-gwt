This test demonstrates several ResizablePanels. Two types of ResizablePanels are created three times to hold a TEXTAREA, IMAGE and SPAN. ResizablePanels with handles always visible appear in the left column whilst those with invisible handles appear in the right column of the grid.
Things to test
  • Text selection is turned off whenever a handle drag starts, letting go of the handle should reenable text selection via dragging of the cursor
  • A handle when being dragged should have an almost black (darkgray) background. The handle colour should return to its original colour when it is let go.
  • Dragging a handle away from the center of the ResizablePanel should increase the width/height of the panel and child widget with dragging inwards decreasing.
  • ResizablePanel's with invisible handles (those in the right column of the grid) only have visible handles when the mouse enters the panel and are hidden once more when the mouse leaves.
  • The handles should appear in the relevant spot half inside or overlapping the child widget and half over the edge.
